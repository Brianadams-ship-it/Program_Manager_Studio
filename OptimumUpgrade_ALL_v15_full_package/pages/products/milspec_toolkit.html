Build Compliant Plans and Reports Faster

The MIL-SPEC & DID Toolkit gives you lean, ready-to-tailor templates and indices modeled on common military standards and Data Item Descriptions (DIDs).

Use it as a starting point for your PMP, ICDs, Test Reports and other contract deliverables.

Included in the Toolkit

  • MIL-SPEC & DID Catalog (PDF)
    MILSPEC_DID_Catalog_OptimumUpgrade.pdf – overview of the standards-style materials included.
  • DID-Style Document Templates
    Located in templates/doc_templates/:
    • PMP_Template.md – Project Management Plan
    • ICD_Template.md – Interface Control Document
    • Test_Report_Template.md – Test Report
  • Office Formatting Spec
    Design_Spec_Office_Formatting.md – how to style the templates in Word and Excel for a consistent, professional look.

How It Connects to the Rest of the Suite

  • Use the templates alongside the Requirements and TestCases sheets from the Automation Suite.
  • Reference Work Breakdown and risk data from the PM/SE starter toolkits.
  • Extend the documents to reference your own MIL-STDs and contract-specific DIDs.
